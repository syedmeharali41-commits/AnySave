                       ANYSAVE ADVANCED — USER MANUAL / HOW TO USE
================================================================================

Welcome to AnySave Advanced! This guide will walk you through the absolute basics 
of downloading your first video, all the way to utilizing the app's most advanced, 
professional-grade features. 

Let's dive in!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 1: THE QUICK-START GUIDE (DOWNLOAD IN 3 EASY STEPS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. COPY A LINK:
   Find the video or audio you want to download on any website (e.g., YouTube, 
   TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X). Copy the URL link from your browser's address 
   bar or from the "Share" menu on mobile/web apps.

2. DETECT & PASTE:
   Open AnySave Advanced. 
   * If "Smart-Paste" is active, AnySave will instantly detect your copied link 
     and show a pop-up prompting you to analyze it.
   * Alternatively, you can click the blue "Paste" button inside the main text 
     input bar, or manually paste it (Ctrl+V) into the input box.

3. DOWNLOAD:
   Click the prominent "DOWNLOAD" button. AnySave will automatically analyze the 
   video formats, grab the highest available quality, and begin downloading it 
   to your selected folder.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 2: ADVANCED CONTROLS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

* Choosing Custom Qualities & Audio:
  When you paste a link, a format selector panel appears on the right. 
  - To download as video: Select "Video" and choose your desired resolution 
    (from 4K UHD, 1080p, 720p, down to 360p).
  - To download as audio: Select "Audio" and pick your format (MP3, AAC, or FLAC) 
    along with your preferred bitrate quality (e.g., 320kbps for maximum quality).

* Extracting Specific Clips (Trimming):
  Before hitting download, look at the format selection area. 
  - Check the "Trim Video" box.
  - Enter the Start Time and End Time (format: HH:MM:SS or just seconds). 
  - Hit Download! AnySave will only extract and download that specific time segment.

* Downloading Entire Playlists:
  Paste a link to a YouTube playlist or channel.
  - A modal will open displaying every video in the playlist.
  - Select or deselect individual videos.
  - Choose to download the entire playlist in bulk or individually tweak settings.

* Skipping Sponsored Video Segments:
  Under the format selector options:
  - Check the box for "SponsorBlock".
  - This instructs AnySave to download the video but slice out annoying sponsors, 
    intro/outro promotions, and non-music filler, saving you time and bandwidth.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 3: UNDERSTANDING THE SETTINGS PANEL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Click the gear icon (Settings) in the bottom-left sidebar to customize your setup:

- Save Folder: 
  Click "Change" next to "Output Folder" to select where your finished files go. 
  You can also easily access your save folder at any time by clicking the hard 
  drive icon at the bottom of the sidebar.
  
- Concurrent Downloads:
  Adjust the slider between 1 and 5. This sets how many downloads can run at the 
  same time. If you have fast internet, push it to 5!

- SponsorBlock Categories:
  Check or uncheck which categories you want SponsorBlock to automatically skip 
  (e.g., sponsors, self-promotion, intros, outros).

- Auto-Paste Clipboard:
  Toggle this ON if you want the app to automatically detect media links when you 
  copy them. Toggle OFF if you prefer a fully manual experience.

- Dark Mode & High Contrast Themes:
  Customize the look of AnySave. Choose from default Dark theme, Light theme, 
  or High Contrast mode.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PART 4: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS & TROUBLESHOOTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: Where are my downloaded files?
A: By default, files are saved in your system's "Downloads/AnySave" folder. 
   You can change this in the Settings panel or click the hard-drive icon at the 
   bottom-left to open the folder immediately.

Q: The video download keeps failing or throws an error.
A: Most issues are resolved by letting the app update its internal downloading 
   engine. AnySave automatically does this on startup, but you can force an update 
   by going to Settings -> Engine Update -> click "Check for Updates".

Q: Do I need to install Python or FFmpeg?
A: No! AnySave comes fully packaged with its own fallback media engines, making 
   it 100% plug-and-play.
